My Connection to Pannekoeken


I eat Pannekoeken every morning, with my special syrup,
which you can discover in the recipes section.
Pannekoek is a dutch dish, and my father, who was born in The Netherlands,
always cooks it. It is mostly called "the dutch pancake", though I believe
pancake is an American Pannekoek.
